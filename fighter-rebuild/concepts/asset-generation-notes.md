# MortalCodex Asset Generation Notes

Initial placeholder assets were generated by `scripts/generate-assets.mjs`.
The current fighter sprites were replaced by high-detail GPT Image 2 sprite
targets and sliced by `scripts/slice-high-detail-sheets.py`.

## Visual intent

- Premium late-SNES / arcade fighting sprite art with detailed anatomy, cel-shaded clothing folds, readable faces, and sharp pixel-art silhouettes.
- Sama uses a compact founder silhouette: dark hair, navy bomber jacket, white shirt, dark trousers, sneakers, and ember/orange tech-energy accents.
- Amodi uses glasses, a short beard, a taller stance, purple research coat, teal/white shirt, dark trousers, and teal/violet energy accents.
- The stage is an original "Byte Boardroom" scene: nighttime skyline, arena panels, and a geometric floor.
- HUD art is intentionally simple and readable so the detailed fighters remain the visual focus.
- Move semantics are fixed for downstream combat and hitbox work: `light` is a punch, `heavy` is a kick, and `special` is a multi-beat combo attack using charge, punch, kick, and finisher frames.

## Clean-room process

- The first pass generator draws primitives directly into RGBA buffers and encodes PNG files in Node.
- The accepted fighter sprite source sheets are saved under `concepts/high-detail-sprite-sheets/`; generated originals remain under the Codex generated-images folder.
- The slicer removes the neutral checkerboard background, crops a 5x2 pose grid, and emits 320x320 Phaser animation strips.
- No reference-app images, sprite sheets, atlas files, prompts, configs, or audio files are read.
- SFX are synthetic placeholder WAV tones generated from deterministic math.
- The contact sheet at `public/assets/concepts/contact-sheet.png` is for visual inspection only.

## Generated scope

- Characters: sama, amodi
- Animations per character: idle, walk, jump, block, light punch, heavy kick, special combo, knockdown
- Stage: byte-boardroom
- Audio keys: sfx-light, sfx-heavy, sfx-block, sfx-special, sfx-hit, sfx-round
